First post

25/01/24 (25th of January)

Being a girl student is like juggling multiple hats while walking on a tightrope. From attending classes to managing assignments, maintaining a social life, and squeezing in some self-care, every day is a whirlwind of activities. The morning starts with a rush to get ready for classes, grabbing a quick breakfast, and dashing out the door with books in hand. The lecture halls become our second home as we immerse ourselves in lessons, taking notes furiously and participating in discussions. Between classes, there's barely a moment to spare as we dash to the library or a nearby café to catch up on assignments or study for upcoming exams. Time management becomes our best friend as we navigate deadlines and prioritize tasks. But it's not all about academics.

Second post

28/01/24 (28th of January)

In the midst of hectic academic schedules and bustling campus life, sometimes all it takes is a short journey to the mountains to find solace and adventure. For this student girl, escaping the confines of textbooks and classrooms meant embarking on a thrilling expedition to conquer the nearby mountain. With a backpack filled with essentials – water, snacks, a map, and a camera – she set off early in the morning, eager to leave the worries of assignments and exams behind. The crisp mountain air greeted her as she began her ascent, the trail winding its way through lush forests and rocky terrain. With each step, she felt a sense of liberation, leaving behind the noise of the city for the serene beauty of nature. The chirping of birds and the rustle of leaves accompanied her journey, reminding her of the simple joys often overlooked in the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
